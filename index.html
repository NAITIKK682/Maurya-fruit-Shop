About Maurya Fruit Shop

Established in 2016, Maurya Fruit Shop has been serving the community with the freshest and highest quality fruits for over three decades. We source our fruits directly from local farmers and APMC market Vashi ( Mumbai ) who practice organic farming methods, ensuring that every fruit you buy is not only delicious but also healthy and sustainable.

Our mission is to bring the taste of nature to your table while supporting local agriculture and sustainable practices. We believe in quality over quantity, and every fruit is hand-selected with care.
